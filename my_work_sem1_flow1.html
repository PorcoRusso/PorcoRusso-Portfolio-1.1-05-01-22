School Projects
Flow 1 | Photo Editing

This Flow was the introduction to many of the programs that we were going to work with in the near future. Our first assignment was to go out in the city and take pictures, that we then had to work with in Adobe Photoshop. Certain elements of the picture needed to be removed and new ones added.

Original Picture

I chose to work with a the picture of an arch with a sunny background taken by the canals of Christianshavn. I thought that this picture had much potential and i had a lot of ideas to start with.

Final Edit

The final edit shows the then sunny Christianhavn turned into an dystopian nightmare as the world is taken over by the Galactic Empire and as stormtroppers are roaming the streets, so are the mulituple fighterjets in the skies.
